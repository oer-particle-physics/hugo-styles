## Lesson motivation

Use this page for the rationale behind the lesson, expected learner background, and the narrative arc you want instructors to keep in mind.

## Teaching tips

- explain the module/template split before the mechanics of shortcodes
- use the all-in-one page when teaching from a single browser tab
- keep challenge prompts small enough that solutions are discussable, not just revealable

## Common trouble spots

- migrated legacy content often still contains custom Jekyll includes
- raw iframe embeds should be converted to supported Hugo shortcodes where possible
- repo-specific branding is usually better handled as a local override than as a shared default
